Ask Us Directly
How does Lady R's property management service work?
+
We take full responsibility for your property — finding and vetting tenants, collecting rent, coordinating maintenance, and sending monthly financial reports. Our management fee is 10% of monthly rent. You receive passive income with zero headaches.
Can diaspora Ghanaians buy property through Lady R remotely?
+
Absolutely. We specialise in serving diaspora clients in the UK, US, Canada, and Europe. We conduct video site tours, handle all documentation including power of attorney, and provide regular updates. Many of our clients have never set foot in a property before purchasing.
What is your commission structure for property sales?
+
Our standard agency commission is 3-5% of the final sale price, paid upon successful completion. For buyers, we offer free buyer representation — our fee is covered by the seller. We are fully transparent about all costs upfront.
How do I know a property has a clean title in Ghana?
+
Every property we list undergoes our rigorous due diligence process — including a Lands Commission title search, site verification, and document authentication. We only present properties with clean, verifiable ownership history.
What areas of Accra do you cover?
+
We cover all of Accra with particular expertise in Airport Residential, East Legon, Cantonments, Labone, Osu, Trasacco Valley, Adjiringanor, Dzorwulu, and Tema. We also operate across Greater Accra and can assist with select regional properties.
How quickly can I get a property valuation?
+
Our instant online tool gives you an estimated range immediately. For a professional valuation with a written report, our team typically completes an assessment within 48-72 hours of a site visit. First-time valuations are free of charge.
What makes Lady R different from other agencies in Accra?
+
18 years of market experience, an award-winning CEO with IFMA membership, genuine care for every client's outcome, specialist expertise in diaspora property management, and a track record of 370+ successful transactions. We don't just sell properties — we build trusted, long-term relationships.
