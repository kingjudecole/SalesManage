Questions & Answers

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about buying, selling, renting, and investing in Ghana real estate with Lady R.

How do I get started with buying a property through Lady R? +

Simply contact us via WhatsApp, call, or submit a contact form. One of our senior agents will schedule a free consultation to understand your needs, budget, and preferred location. We'll then shortlist properties and arrange viewings — all at no cost to you. Our commission is paid by the seller, not the buyer.

Can I buy property in Ghana if I live abroad? +

Yes — and this is one of our specialities. Our Diaspora Investment Package is designed for Ghanaians in the UK, US, Canada, and beyond who want to invest without travelling home. We handle the property search, due diligence, inspections, legal documentation, and ongoing management entirely on your behalf. Everything is communicated via video call, email, and WhatsApp.

How long does it typically take to sell a property? +

For well-priced properties in prime Accra locations, our average time to sale is 30–90 days. The key factors are competitive pricing (which our free valuation ensures), professional photography, and our extensive buyer network. Properties priced correctly and marketed effectively through Lady R sell significantly faster than the market average.

What does property management include? +

Our full property management service includes: tenant sourcing and vetting, lease agreement preparation, rent collection, routine and emergency maintenance coordination, property inspections, monthly financial reporting, and tenant communication. We charge 10% of the monthly rent collected — you receive the remaining 90% without lifting a finger.

Are property prices in Ghana quoted in GHC or USD? +

Properties are legally transacted in Ghanaian Cedis (GHC), but many sellers and developers list prices in USD as a hedge against currency fluctuation. We show both currencies on all our listings for transparency. Final transaction value depends on the agreed currency between buyer and seller, and we can advise on the most advantageous approach.

What documents do I need when buying property in Ghana? +

Key documents include: valid Ghana card or passport, TIN (Tax Identification Number), proof of funds, and for final registration — a signed sale agreement and indenture. Our documentation team handles all land registry searches, title verification, and paperwork preparation. We ensure there are zero legal surprises before you sign.

How do I know a property has a clean title in Ghana? +

This is the most important question in Ghanaian real estate. Every property we list undergoes our mandatory due diligence process: land registry search at the Lands Commission, family and traditional authority checks, boundary verification, and site inspection. We will never list or facilitate the sale of a property with a disputed or unclear title.

Does Lady R handle short-term/Airbnb rentals? +

Yes. Our short-term rental management service handles everything — listing creation and optimisation on Airbnb and Booking.com, guest screening and communication, professional cleaning coordination, pricing strategy, and monthly revenue reporting. We typically achieve 25–40% higher income for clients compared to long-term rental.
