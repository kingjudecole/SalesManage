Our Story

Wholly Ghanaian.
Globally Trusted.

Founded in 2008 in the heart of Accra's Airport Residential Area, Lady R Real Estate Services Ltd was born from a singular conviction: that every Ghanaian deserves to be treated as royalty when it comes to their most important asset.

Our founder, Mrs. Regina Christian Koomson — a Member of IFMA and an internationally recognised real estate leader — built Lady R on the pillars of expertise, passion, and unwavering integrity. Today, with a team of 21 specialists and over 370 successful transactions, we are not just Ghana's premier agency; we are the gold standard.
